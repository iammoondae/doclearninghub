# Chairperson Executive Center Architecture Blueprint

See architecture summarized below.

## Vision

Executive layer above Student, Faculty, and Laboratory modules.
This will change as modules change or created.

## Modules

-   Executive Dashboard
-   Academic Affairs
-   Faculty Oversight
-   Laboratory Oversight
-   Student Oversight
-   Strategic Planning
-   Executive Reports
-   Executive Approvals
-   Executive Calendar
-   Executive Settings

## Key Principles

-   Read-only dashboard by default.
-   Operational work stays in source modules.
-   Role switching (Student, Faculty, Laboratory only).
-   Shared services: notifications, approvals, digital signatures,
    reports, analytics, search.
