# Chairperson Executive Center Redesign Plan (Updated)

## Vision
Executive command layer for the Department of Chemistry Digital Operations Platform.

## Executive Dashboard
- Department Health Index (configurable)
- KPI Cards
- Pending Approvals
- Executive Calendar
- Notifications

## Academic Governance
- Academic Affairs
- Curriculum Management (Coming Soon / Greyed Out)
- Faculty Oversight (teaching, consultation, research, extension, accreditation, instructional materials, attendance, evaluations, accomplishment reports)
- Student Oversight

## Operational Governance
- Laboratory Oversight
- PCO/EMIS Oversight (office-generated hazardous waste only; chemistry laboratory hazardous waste remains under the Laboratory/Stockroom module)

## Strategic Governance
- Strategic Planning (VMGO, 5-Year Plan, AOP, Procurement, Faculty Development, Laboratory Development, Risk Register, KPI Monitoring)
- Executive Reports grouped into Faculty, Laboratory, PCO/EMIS, Student and Executive Reports
- Executive Calendar (recurring events, tasks, reminders, attachments)

## Decision Center
- Executive Approvals (procurement, curriculum, leave, laboratory purchases, equipment disposal, chemical purchases, documents)
- Executive Notifications

## Executive Settings
- Dashboard
- Notifications
- Digital Signature

## Department Health Index
Configurable weights. Suggested defaults:
- Student Progress 35%
- Faculty Performance 20%
- Laboratory Operations 20%
- Environmental Compliance 15%
- Strategic Planning 10%

## Shared Services
Notifications, Approvals, Digital Signatures, Analytics, Reports, Calendar, Search, Audit Logs, Document Repository.

## Long-Term Vision
Department Command Center.
